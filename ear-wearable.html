Low-Power Ear Wearable Prototype

Designed and engineered by Athlete Engineering as a collaborative team effort.

Developed an ear-based wearable device for continuous temperature and environmental monitoring in demanding work environments. My role focused on hardware integration and implementing robust data transfer protocols.

Technical Overview

  • Circuitpython firmware for reliable sensor management
  • Custom PCB design for compact integration
  • BME680 sensor for multi-parameter environmental data
  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for wireless configuration and monitoring
  • USB-C UART protocol for fast, secure data transfer
  • Python-based GUI for real-time device control and data access
  • Efficient minute-level logging for long-duration shifts
